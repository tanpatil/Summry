Summry is a web app that helps you condense lengthy audio lectures and transcripts into manageable bullet-point summaries. With online education becoming increasingly popular, there are millions of hours of Zoom lectures available. However, sifting through all of this material to find what is relevant for exams can be both boring and tedious. Summry offers a solution by summarizing hour-long videos into 1-page study guides, allowing students to review lecture material more conveniently, comprehensively, and efficiently.
